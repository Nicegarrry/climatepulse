1. energy-generation: Power generation from any source — solar, wind, hydro, gas, coal, nuclear — including new projects, closures, capacity additions, generation records, and equipment manufacturing.
Key terms: solar farm, wind farm, GW capacity, power station, coal closure, nuclear, SMR, turbine, panel manufacturing, generation record.
Not: Battery storage (energy-storage), transmission lines (energy-grid), electricity pricing (finance-investment).

2. energy-storage: Energy storage technologies and deployments — grid-scale batteries, home batteries, pumped hydro for storage, hydrogen as energy storage, thermal and mechanical storage.
Key terms: BESS, battery, MWh, sodium-ion, lithium-ion, pumped hydro, VPP, home battery, flow battery, long-duration storage.
Not: Pumped hydro as generation asset (energy-generation), battery minerals extraction (critical-minerals), EV batteries (transport).

3. energy-grid: Electricity transmission, distribution, grid connection, grid stability, electricity market design, demand response, microgrids, and distributed energy resource integration.
Key terms: transmission, NEM, AEMO, grid connection, interconnector, distribution network, REZ, demand response, frequency control, market reform, DER, microgrid.
Not: Generation assets (energy-generation), storage assets (energy-storage), electricity pricing as financial product (finance-investment).

4. carbon-emissions: Carbon pricing, emissions trading systems, carbon border adjustments, voluntary and compliance carbon markets, carbon offsets, carbon removal technologies, CCS, and emissions measurement.
Key terms: ETS, carbon price, CBAM, ACCU, offset, Safeguard Mechanism, carbon credit, DAC, CCS, emissions, MRV, sequestration.
Not: Emissions from specific sectors (tag the sector domain), ESG reporting frameworks (finance-investment).

5. transport: Decarbonisation of transport — electric vehicles, charging infrastructure, fleet electrification, hydrogen transport, aviation and maritime decarbonisation, rail, and alternative fuels.
Key terms: EV, electric vehicle, charging, fleet, SAF, sustainable aviation fuel, e-fuel, hydrogen truck, shipping decarbonisation, battery electric, fuel cell.
Not: EV battery manufacturing (critical-minerals if minerals, energy-storage if battery tech).

6. industry: Decarbonisation of heavy industry — steel, cement, aluminium, chemicals, mining, LNG/gas upstream, data centres, advanced manufacturing, waste processing, and circular economy.
Key terms: green steel, DRI, cement, aluminium smelter, chemical plant, mining electrification, data centre, LNG, fugitive emissions, waste-to-energy, circular economy, industrial heat.
Not: Industrial company financial results (finance-investment), industrial policy/regulation (policy-governance).

7. agriculture-land: Agriculture emissions, livestock methane, alternative proteins, regenerative farming, soil carbon, forestry, deforestation, land clearing, water management, and food systems.
Key terms: methane, livestock, Asparagopsis, alternative protein, regenerative, soil carbon, reforestation, deforestation, food waste, Murray-Darling.
Not: Bioenergy for power generation (energy-generation), agricultural carbon credits as financial instruments (carbon-emissions).

8. built-environment: Building energy efficiency, electrification of buildings, embodied carbon in construction, green building standards, sustainable precincts, and urban heat management.
Key terms: building efficiency, heat pump, NABERS, Green Star, NatHERS, embodied carbon, retrofit, induction, gas-to-electric, sustainable precinct, urban heat.
Not: Construction of energy infrastructure (relevant energy domain), building materials as industrial products (industry).

9. critical-minerals: Extraction, processing, and supply chains for minerals critical to the energy transition — lithium, rare earths, cobalt, nickel, graphite, copper. Includes recycling and supply chain diversification.
Key terms: lithium, rare earth, cobalt, nickel, graphite, copper, DLE, refining, supply chain, friend-shoring, battery recycling, panel recycling.
Not: Mining companies' general corporate news (finance-investment), mine site electrification (industry).

10. finance-investment: Financial flows into and around the energy transition — project finance, green bonds, ESG disclosure, greenwashing enforcement, climate risk, insurance, M&A, and electricity contract markets.
Key terms: project finance, green bond, PPA, ESG, ISSB, TCFD, greenwashing, climate risk, insurance, stranded asset, M&A, investment, offtake.
Not: Carbon credit pricing (carbon-emissions), government funding programs as policy (policy-governance).

11. policy-governance: Government policy, legislation, and governance frameworks — federal and state energy/climate policy, international agreements, environmental approvals, community engagement, Indigenous engagement, and corporate governance.
Key terms: federal policy, state policy, legislation, approval, EPBC Act, Paris Agreement, COP, NDC, community engagement, social licence, Indigenous, directors' duties.
Not: Regulatory enforcement (could be finance-investment if greenwashing, carbon-emissions if offsets).

12. workforce-adaptation: The human and environmental dimensions — energy workforce, skills gaps, just transition, climate science, physical adaptation, biodiversity, extreme weather, health, and cleantech R&D.
Key terms: workforce, skills gap, apprenticeship, just transition, CSIRO, climate science, adaptation, biodiversity, extreme weather, bushfire, flood, heatwave, R&D.
Not: Labour costs as project finance (finance-investment), workforce policy announcements (policy-governance if focus is the policy itself).
